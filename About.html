About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Nebraska in Omaha (May 2023). Art and programming have been my passion for nearly a decade now. I first started art journery in elementary school and later picked up programming as a junior in high school. Making this site was a great way to combine both my passions and will continue to fuel my learning. 

I come from a large christian family in Nebraska. Being the youngest of 6 siblings I have had a lot of people to look up to and learn from. I look to continue building on the groundwork my upbringing has provided me. 

On my free time, I hike, do art, fix broken tech and occasional play/create video games. Little projects like this one keep my mind sharpened. So I can keep what I learned fresh in my mind.
